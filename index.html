Augument Reality

Real-world integration

AR overlays digital objects onto the real environment, making virtual content interact naturally with what we see around us.

Real-time interaction

AR responds instantly to user movements and actions, creating an interactive and immersive experience.

Hardware and software support

Devices like smartphones, AR glasses, cameras, sensors, and AR platforms are essential for accurate tracking and smooth performance.

Virtual Reality

VR Hardware

Devices like VR headsets, controllers, sensors, and headphones that allow users to see, hear, and interact in a virtual world.

Software / Virtual Environment

The computer-generated 3D world, including apps, games, or simulations that users experience in VR.

Tracking & Interaction System

Technology that tracks head, hand, and body movements so actions feel natural and immersive.

Blockchain Technology

Decentralization

Data is not controlled by a single authority; it is shared across many computers (nodes).

Transparency

All transactions are recorded on a public ledger that can be viewed and verified by participants.

Security & Immutability

Once data is added to the blockchain, it is very difficult to change, making it secure and trustworthy.

Artificial Technology

Machine Learning

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Computer Vision
